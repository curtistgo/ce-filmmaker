Curtis

The Filmmaker

Curtis is a New York City based filmmaker who studied the art of dramatic writing, acting, and filmmaking at Stony Brook University. He continued his grad work at New School University. Coming from a social science background, he enjoys writing stories that relate to social issues. The stories don't have to be heavy. He just likes to play around with issues that affect us all.
